Arabic character notes

Contents

This page lists characters used in orthographies based on the Arabic script and provides information about them.

Character notes have companion pages that summarise how a script is used for a specific orthography. For the Arabic script, see Adamawa Fulfulde (Ajamiya), Hausa (Warsh Kano), Persian (Arabic), Kashmiri (Nastaliq Arabic), Modern Standard Arabic, Urdu (Nastaliq Arabic), and Uighur (Arabic).
