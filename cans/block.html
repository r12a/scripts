UCAS character notes

Contents

This page lists characters used in orthographies based on the Unified Canadian Aboriginal Syllabics (UCAS) script and provides information about them.

Character notes have companion pages that summarise how a script is used for a specific orthography. For the UCAS script, see Plains Cree (UCAS), and Inuktitut (UCAS).
