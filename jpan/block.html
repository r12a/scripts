Japanese character notes

Contents

This page lists characters used in orthographies based on the Japanese script and provides information about them. The Japanese script is composed of four subscripts: Kanji, Hiragana, Katakana, & Latin.

Character notes have companion pages that summarise how a script is used for a specific orthography. For the Japanese script, see Japanese (Kanji, Hiragana, Katakana, & Latin).
