Latin character notes

Contents

This page lists characters used in orthographies based on the Latin script and provides information about them. It also includes punctuation and combining characters common to many orthographies.

Character notes have companion pages that summarise how a script is used for a specific orthography. For the Latin script, see Fula (Latin), Bamanan (Latin), Hausa (Latin), and Wolof (Latin).
