Tagbanwa character notes

Contents

This page lists characters used in orthographies based on the Tagbanwa script and provides information about them.

Character notes have companion pages that summarise how a script is used for a specific orthography. For the Tagbanwa script, see Aborlan Tagbanwa.

The examples used in this page are constructed from Latin transcriptions, following the rules of the script. It is possible that one or two items may be incorrect.
